Waveroom is a browser-based recording studio for podcasts, interviews, and remote conversations. As Principal Designer, I shaped the product end to end, focusing on a workflow that made high-quality local recording feel simple, reliable, and accessible.

I built a token-based design system that supported the product across web, iOS, and desktop, while also creating the brand platform from scratch: positioning, tone of voice, and visual guidelines. The goal was to give the product a stronger point of view without sacrificing clarity.

Alongside the interface work, I led and mentored designers and introduced DesignOps practices that made the team more consistent as the product evolved. The result was a tighter system around recording, collaboration, and AI-assisted cleanup.
