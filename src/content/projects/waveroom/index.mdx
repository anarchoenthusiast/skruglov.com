Waveroom had to make a technically demanding recording workflow feel simple, reliable, and accessible in the browser. As Principal Designer, I shaped the product end to end for podcasters, interviewers, and teams recording remote conversations.

I built the product experience around local recording, collaboration, and AI-assisted cleanup, then created the system that kept those surfaces consistent across web, iOS, and desktop. The work also included the brand platform from scratch: positioning, tone of voice, and visual guidelines.

Alongside the interface work, I led and mentored designers and introduced DesignOps practices that made the team more consistent as the product evolved. The result was a clearer product foundation with a stronger point of view and less friction around a complex recording flow.
