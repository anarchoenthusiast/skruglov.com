INTERVALS FESTIVAL is a large-scale multimedia art festival in Nizhny Novgorod, built around the meeting point of technology, sound, visual culture, and space. It stands out because the city itself becomes part of the experience.

The public reference for this project places it in the 2019-2021 period. The current festival platform presents Intervals as an international program spanning exhibitions, education, music, and performance, with a strong emphasis on media art as a spatial and cultural system.
